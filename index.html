About US

The college was established in 1998 and offers B.Tech Courses in nine disciplines of Engineering.
The college also offers M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering.



Campus Overview



Modern College campus provides an ideal environment for young minds to explore new ideas, encourage creativity and independent thinking.
It facilitates faculty to engage in research, staff to cultivate empathy towards students, and everyone to develop the twin qualities of life-long learning for yearning for learning.
